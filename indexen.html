China's aging problems and challenges

The pace of population aging in China is increasing dramatically. According to Bureau of Statistics, today, there are more than 200 million people are aged 60 or older. The ratio of elderly will reach to 64 for every 100 workers by 2025. By 2050, the total number of this population will reach to 430 million, which mean every 1 in 3 people in China will be an elderly.

The successful elder care model in China will need to be accepted by both those paying for the care, as well as those receiving it, to meet the cultural expectations of how seniors are to be honored and taken care of in their old age.
